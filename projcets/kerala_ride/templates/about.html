About KeralaRide Connect

Our mission is to build Kerala's most trusted, unified transport marketplace.

Our Vision

KeralaRide Connect was established to solve a unique challenge: connecting passenger commutes and goods transport under one transparent regional platform. By collaborating with local bodies (panchayats, municipalities, and corporations), we make sure commercial vehicle operations run in harmony with community safety guidelines.

From Trivandrum to Kasaragod, we support Kerala's local drivers by giving them a digital portal to find jobs without middleman commissions, keeping fares affordable for customers.

Supported Vehicle Network

  • Auto Rickshaw: Short commutes in urban and village lanes
  • Taxi / Standard Cars: Inter-city comfort rides
  • MPV / Innova: Premium 7-seater family tourism
  • Tempo Traveller & Bus: Group tourism and events
  • Pickup & Lorry: Light and heavy freight shipment
